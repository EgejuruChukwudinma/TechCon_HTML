History

TechCon began as a small community meetup and has grown into a global conference where engineers, designers, researchers, and founders share breakthroughs in software, hardware, and product development.

Mission

Our mission is to accelerate innovation by connecting curious minds, sharing practical knowledge, and celebrating open collaboration across the tech ecosystem.
